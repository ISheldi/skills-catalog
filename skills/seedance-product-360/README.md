# seedance-product-360

Generate product 360° turntable, multi-angle showcase, and product reveal video prompts for Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield. Use whenever the user wants a product rotation video, turntable showcase, product reveal, 360 view, multi-angle product display, product beauty shot, hero product video, or unboxing reveal. Triggers on: product 360, turntable, product rotation, multi-angle, product reveal, product showcase, hero shot, beauty shot, product spin, unboxing, or any request to showcase a physical product from multiple angles. Use even for "show my product from all sides" or "make a product video.

## Установка
```bash
git clone https://github.com/yasikvlad/seedance-product-360 ~/.claude/skills/seedance-product-360
```
